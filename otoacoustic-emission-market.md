<p><p>Otoacoustic emission (OAE) refers to the sounds generated by the inner ear when the hair cells in the cochlea respond to sound stimulation. These emissions can be used to assess hearing function, particularly in newborns, where traditional hearing tests may not be feasible. The OAE testing is non-invasive, quick, and often employed in early detection of hearing impairments or auditory dysfunctions.</p><p>The Otoacoustic Emission Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of hearing-related issues and advancements in diagnostic technologies. Rising prevalence of hearing disorders, particularly in the aging population, and a growing emphasis on early screening programs are key factors contributing to market expansion.
