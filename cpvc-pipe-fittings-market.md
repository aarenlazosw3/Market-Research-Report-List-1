<p><p>The CPVC pipe and fittings market comprises two primary segments: CPVC pipes and CPVC fittings. CPVC pipes are designed for high-temperature and pressure applications, typically used in residential, commercial, and industrial plumbing systems. They are resistant to corrosion and suitable for transporting hot and cold water. CPVC fittings, on the other hand, are the connectors that facilitate the joining of CPVC pipes, ensuring a secure and leak-proof system. Both components are essential for efficient fluid handling and distribution, standing out for their durability and versatility.</p></p>
