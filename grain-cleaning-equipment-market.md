<p><p>The grain cleaning equipment market can be divided into two main types: pre-cleaning and fine cleaning. Pre-cleaning equipment is designed to remove large debris and impurities before the grains undergo further processing, enhancing efficiency in subsequent cleaning stages. Fine cleaning equipment, on the other hand, focuses on the removal of smaller particles and impurities, ensuring the final grain product meets quality standards. Both types are essential for optimizing grain quality and increasing overall processing effectiveness in the agricultural sector.</p></p>
