<p><p>The High Pressure Roll Compactor market features two primary types: briquetting and granulation. Briquetting involves compressing fine powders into solid, dense shapes known as briquettes, which are often used for fuel or raw material in various industries. Granulation, on the other hand, focuses on forming granules from powders, enhancing their flow properties and improving handling. Both processes utilize high pressure to achieve desired particle size and density, serving applications in pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and materials processing.</p></p>
