<p><strong>The PET Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BoPET</li><li>CPET</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET film market primarily comprises two types: BoPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) and CPET (Crystallized Polyethylene Terephthalate). BoPET is known for its excellent tensile strength, high clarity, and barrier properties, making it ideal for packaging and electrical applications. In contrast, CPET is characterized by its increased crystallinity, offering enhanced thermal resistance and stability. Both types serve diverse industries, including packaging, electronics, and automotive, providing tailored solutions based on their unique properties.</p></p>
