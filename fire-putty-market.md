<p><strong>The Fire Putty Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastometric</li><li>Intumescent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fire Putty market comprises two main types: elastomeric and intumescent. Elastomeric fire putty provides flexible fire resistance by forming a barrier that expands under heat, sealing gaps in fire-rated assemblies. In contrast, intumescent fire putty expands significantly when exposed to high temperatures, creating an insulating char that protects against flames and smoke. Both types are essential in various construction and wiring applications to enhance fire safety while accommodating movement and structural integrity in buildings.</p></p>
