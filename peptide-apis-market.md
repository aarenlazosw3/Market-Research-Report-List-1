<p><p>The peptide APIs market is primarily divided into two types: Liquid Phase Peptide Synthesis (LPPS) and Solid Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS). LPPS involves synthesizing peptides in a liquid solution, allowing for longer peptide chains and complex structures, making it suitable for certain applications. In contrast, SPPS synthesizes peptides on a solid support, enabling high purity and efficient automation. Both methods cater to various pharmaceutical needs, including drug development and therapeutic applications, driven by advancements in peptide technology.</p></p>
