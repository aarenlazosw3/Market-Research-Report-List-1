<p><p>The high strength steel (HSS) market is divided into two main types: Conventional High Strength Steel (HSS) and Advanced High Strength Steel (AHSS). Conventional HSS offers increased strength over standard steel, suitable for applications requiring improved load-bearing capability without significantly increasing weight. In contrast, AHSS combines various alloys and processing techniques to achieve greater strength, ductility, and formability, enabling innovative designs and lightweight structures in industries like automotive and construction, where safety and efficiency are paramount.</p></p>
