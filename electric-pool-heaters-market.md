<p><ul><li>Constant Temperature Furnace</li><li>Heat Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric pool heater market primarily includes constant temperature furnaces and heat pumps. Constant temperature furnaces provide consistent heating by maintaining a set water temperature, ensuring user comfort and convenience. Heat pumps, on the other hand, utilize ambient air or ground temperature to heat the pool water efficiently, making them more energy-efficient in mild climates. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with furnaces offering quick heating and heat pumps promoting long-term energy savings.</p></p>
