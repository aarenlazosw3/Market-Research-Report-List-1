<p><p>The online classroom market is primarily segmented into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based classrooms use internet resources for hosting and can be accessed from anywhere, providing flexibility and scalability for institutions. They often come with subscription models and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises solutions are installed locally on the institution's servers, offering greater control over data and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves different institutional needs and strategic goals.</p></p>
