<p><strong>The ECG Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resting ECG</li><li>Stress ECG</li><li>Holter Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECG monitoring system market is categorized into three main types: Resting ECG, Stress ECG, and Holter Monitors. Resting ECGs capture the heart's electrical activity in a stable, relaxed state, primarily used for diagnostics. Stress ECGs assess heart function during physical exertion, vital for detecting exercise-induced cardiac issues. Holter Monitors are portable devices worn for 24-48 hours, providing continuous monitoring of heart rhythms over time, offering insights into arrhythmias and other abnormalities during normal daily activities.</p></p>
