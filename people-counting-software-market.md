<p><p>People counting software utilizes various technologies to monitor foot traffic in spaces. IR Beam systems use infrared sensors to detect the presence of individuals by breaking an infrared beam, suitable for simple entrances. Thermal imaging systems detect body heat, offering accuracy even in low light or crowded areas. Video-based counting employs computer vision algorithms to analyze footage from cameras, providing in-depth analytics and useful insights into visitor behavior, making it ideal for larger and complex environments.</p></p>
