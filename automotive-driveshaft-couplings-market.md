<p><p>The automotive driveshaft couplings market encompasses various types designed to transmit torque while accommodating misalignment and vibration. Mechanical element flexible shaft couplings use flexible elements to connect shafts, allowing for some movement. Elastomeric element couplings utilize rubber or polymer materials for shock absorption and flexibility. Metallic membrane or disc type couplings employ thin metal discs that flex to accommodate misalignment while maintaining high torque transmission efficiency. Each type offers distinct advantages for performance, durability, and application suitability in vehicles.</p></p>
