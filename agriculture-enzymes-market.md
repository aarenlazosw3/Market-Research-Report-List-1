<p><p>The agriculture enzymes market encompasses various types, including phosphatases, dehydrogenases, proteases, and sulfatases, each playing a crucial role in enhancing soil health and plant growth. Phosphatases facilitate phosphorus availability, essential for energy transfer and photosynthesis. Dehydrogenases are indicators of microbial activity in soil, promoting organic matter decomposition. Proteases aid in protein breakdown, releasing nutrients for plant uptake. Sulfatases contribute to sulfur availability, vital for amino acid synthesis. Together, these enzymes support sustainable agricultural practices and improve crop yields.</p></p>
