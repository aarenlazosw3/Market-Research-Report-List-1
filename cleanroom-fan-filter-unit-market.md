FFUs with HEPA filters are designed to capture particles as small as 0.3 microns, making them suitable for many cleanroom applications. In contrast, FFUs with ULPA filters provide a higher level of filtration, capturing particles as small as 0.1 microns, making them ideal for environments requiring stringent cleanliness standards. Both types ensure optimal air quality in controlled settings.</p></p>
