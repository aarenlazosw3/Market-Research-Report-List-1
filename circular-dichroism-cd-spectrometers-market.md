The market is segmented into three types: linearly polarized light sources, which produce light oscillating in a single plane; circularly polarized light sources, which generate light that rotates in circular motion, enhancing sensitivity to chiral substances; and multiple light sources, combining both linear and circular polarization to provide versatile analysis capabilities. Each type serves distinct applications in structural biology and materials science.</p></p>
