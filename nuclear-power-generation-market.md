<p><p>Nuclear power generation involves various reactor types that influence market dynamics. Pressurized Water Reactors (PWR) use pressurized water to transfer heat, while Boiling Water Reactors (BWR) allow water to boil, generating steam directly. Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWR) utilize heavy water for better efficiency and fuel flexibility. Gas Cooled Reactors (GCR) use gas as a coolant, enhancing safety and efficiency. Additionally, other reactor technologies, including small modular reactors (SMRs) and advanced reactors, are emerging, diversifying the nuclear energy landscape and market opportunities.</p></p>
