<p><p>Digital isolators are critical components in modern electronics, ensuring signal integrity while providing electrical isolation. The market for these devices encompasses three primary types: </p><p>1. **Capacitive Coupling**: Employs capacitors to transfer signals across isolation barriers, offering high data rates and low power consumption.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Magnetic Coupling**: Uses transformer-like magnetic fields for isolation, known for robustness and reliability in noisy environments.</p><p>3. **Giant Magnetoresistive (GMR)**: Utilizes GMR effects for isolation, providing sensitive signal processing with excellent performance in compact designs. These technologies are essential for diverse applications, including industrial automation and telecommunications.</p></p>
