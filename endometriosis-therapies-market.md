<p><p>The endometriosis therapies market comprises several treatment categories aimed at managing symptoms and slowing disease progression. Hormonal contraceptives regulate menstrual cycles and reduce pain. Gonadotropin-releasing hormone (GnRH) agonists inhibit estrogen production, leading to a temporary menopause-like state that alleviates symptoms. Progestin therapy works by thickening endometrial tissue, minimizing bleeding and pain. Aromatase inhibitors lower estrogen levels, helping to shrink endometrial lesions. Together, these therapies offer diverse options for managing endometriosis effectively.</p></p>
