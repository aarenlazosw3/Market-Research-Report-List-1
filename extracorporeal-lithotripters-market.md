<p><p>Extracorporeal lithotripters are medical devices designed to treat kidney stones and other forms of urinary calculi non-invasively. They utilize shock waves generated outside the body to break down stones into smaller fragments, which can then be easily passed through the urinary tract. This non-surgical approach offers numerous advantages, including reduced recovery time and minimal risk of complications, which makes it a preferred option for patients.</p><p>The Extracorporeal Lithotripters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.80% during the forecast period. Additionally, the growing aging population is likely to drive demand for effective treatment options, as older adults are more susceptible to such conditions.</p><p>Recent trends in the market highlight the integration of sophisticated imaging techniques, enhancing the precision of lithotripsy procedures. Innovations such as mobile lithotripsy devices and robotic assistance are also emerging, improving patient safety and treatment efficiency.
