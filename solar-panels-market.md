<p><p>The solar panel market is primarily divided into two types: crystalline silicon solar panels and thin-film solar panels. Crystalline silicon panels, known for their high efficiency and durability, dominate the market due to their widespread adoption and significant energy conversion rates. Thin-film solar panels, on the other hand, are lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for specific applications where space and weight are concerns. However, they typically have lower efficiency and shorter lifespan compared to crystalline silicon alternatives, shaping their respective market niches.</p></p>
