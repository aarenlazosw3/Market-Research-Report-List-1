<p><p>The 3D Solid-state LiDAR market is primarily categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **MEMS Based Scanning**: Utilizes Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems for rapid, precise scanning, ideal for automotive applications.</p><p>2. **Phase Array**: Employs multiple laser beams simultaneously for real-time data capture without mechanical movement, enhancing speed and reliability.</p><p>3. **Non-Scanning Flash**: Captures entire images in a single flash, delivering high-density 3D data quickly, suited for applications requiring instant surface mapping, such as robotics and security systems.</p></p>
